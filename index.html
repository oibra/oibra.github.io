Spaghetti Cacio e Pepe

Time: 25 minutes

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
  2. Cook spaghetti in boiling water until tender but firm to the bite, about 10 minutes. Reserve some of the cooking water and drain spaghetti.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add garlic and pepper to the oil. Cook and stir until fragrant, 1-2 minutes
  5. Add spaghetti and Pecorino Romano cheese
  6. Add ~1/2 cup o reserved pasta water; stir until cheese is melted, about 1 minute.
  7. Add more pata water until the sauce coats the spaghetti, about 1 minute more
